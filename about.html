About Domicile Designs

Ben Woynich

Domicile Designs is a design-build remodeling company that serves the Twin Cities area. Specializing in all parts of the home, including basements, kitchens, bathrooms, living rooms, bedroom, and bonus living spaces.

Your home is an extension of yourself. We can create environments that pair with functionality and creative style. Maybe you like what you already have, we can match and shine up what may be in need of repairs.

With nearly 25 years of experience, rest assured your vision will come to light. Your home says many things and we are able to listen. We understand vintage construction and what it means to stay standing for many decades to come. Detail orientated to get the most out of product warranties and assurance your home is safe.

Collaborating with local respected trades we can manage your projects from start to finish, with only you to enjoy the progress!
